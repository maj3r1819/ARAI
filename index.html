Nature is not a place to visit. It is home.

My name is Sagar and this is my website where I have tried to document my weekend getaways to ARAI which is a nature hike trail near my house. I am always accompanied by my friends Siraj, Soham (Bote) and Siraj's dog Ferro and sometimes by SD. This place is a treat to our mind and relaxes us from the city ruckus. It is always a treat to witness the changing seasons throughout the year. Please scroll below to enjoy our journey so far.

Date: 4th April 2021

This was one of the rare times we visited here in the morning. Surprisingly it wasn't that hot as we expected. We planned to go out for breakfast in the morning but unfortunately all the breakfast joints were shut because of the weekend lockdown. Sadly this was probably the last time this year we could go here. We are waiting desparately for the lockdown to end and for things to get better.

Date: 28th March 2021

I took this just before what we think was a "Paranormal Encounter". It was pitch dark and we don't usually see anyone at such hours. To our disregard we believe we were chased by a lady who was sprinting at the same pace as were running. It was definitely some experience we never had before.

Date: 30th January 2021

One of the better sunsets I have witnessed here. Thanks to the abundant rainfall we received last year, the level of the quarry water did not fall too low. This is a prime spot and is mostly crowded. Luckily I got a good view away from the crowd!

Date: 24th January 2021

Tbh this does not seem like a very good picture. But I took it because I liked the contrast of the colours, ranging from blue to yellowish-orange and how the image gradually got darker at the bottom. Also the water was quite calm at the time.

Date: 17th January 2021

We love to see the moon even when there is still light around. I doubt there's anything more relaxing than clear sky and the wide abyss.
